Project Overview

Problem

Coffee businesses need comprehensive sales insights across products, time periods, and geographic regions to optimize inventory and marketing strategies.

Approach

Built an end-to-end Excel solution using advanced functions (XLOOKUP, INDEX MATCH), data cleaning, and interactive pivot tables with slicers for dynamic filtering.

Solution

Created an interactive dashboard with pivot charts and timeline controls, enabling stakeholders to explore sales trends by coffee type, roast, customer, and country.

Impact

Identified Liberica as highest revenue generator overall, while revealing regional preferences: Arabica in US, Liberica in Ireland, and Robusta in UK.

Key Insights

  • Product Performance: Liberica coffee generates the highest overall revenue, highlighting its strong market appeal
  • Roast Preferences: Light roast is the top revenue contributor across all markets
  • Regional Variations: Clear geographic preferences emerged - Arabica leads in the US, Liberica dominates in Ireland, and Robusta drives sales in the UK
  • Customer Loyalty: Analysis of loyalty card members reveals patterns in repeat purchase behavior

Data Processing Workflow

  • Data Cleaning: Identified and addressed null values, blanks, and errors; removed duplicates and irrelevant columns
  • Data Validation: Ensured consistency in data types and formats across all fields
  • Data Enrichment: Used XLOOKUP and INDEX MATCH to pull customer details (name, email, country) and product information (coffee type, roast, size, unit price)
  • Feature Engineering: Created calculated "Sales" column and added "Coffee Type Name" and "Roast Type Name" using IF functions
  • Pivot Analysis: Built three comprehensive pivot tables examining sales by time, customer, and geography

Detailed Findings

United States Market

Top Performer: Arabica coffee leads in revenue generation, aligning with traditional American coffee preferences for smooth, mild flavors.

Ireland Market

Top Performer: Liberica coffee significantly outperforms other types, suggesting unique taste preferences in the Irish market.

United Kingdom Market

Top Performer: Robusta is the primary revenue driver, indicating preference for stronger, more robust coffee flavors.

Business Recommendations

  • Regional Marketing: Tailor marketing campaigns to highlight preferred coffee types in each market
  • Inventory Optimization: Adjust stock levels based on regional preferences (more Arabica for US, Liberica for Ireland, Robusta for UK)
  • Product Development: Consider developing light roast variations of regional favorites to maximize the "Light" roast trend
  • Customer Loyalty: Focus retention efforts on top 5 customers who represent significant revenue concentration
